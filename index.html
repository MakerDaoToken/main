DYCO (Dynamic Coin Offering): DYCO, also known as Dynamic Coin Offering, offers participants the ability to return purchased tokens. USDC supports 100% of the circulating supply until the refund process is complete.

Investors are not at risk of the token falling at all. strong price. The refunded tokens will be automatically burned, reducing the circulating supply by up to 100%. Dyco was created to encourage serious projects with the tokens they create.
